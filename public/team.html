Online Chatbot-Based Ticketing System

Description

Background: Visitors to museums often face challenges with manual ticket booking systems, including inefficiencies, long queues, and errors such as incorrect ticket issuance and double bookings. These issues can negatively impact the visitor experience and the museum's reputation.

Solution Description: Implementing a chatbot for ticket booking can address these issues by providing:

  • Improved Customer Service
  • Efficient Handling of High Volumes
  • Cost-Effective Solution
  • Data Collection and Analysis
  • Accessibility
  • Reduced Human Error
  • Multilingual Support
  • Enhanced Marketing and Promotion

Expected Solution: A responsive, multilingual chatbot system capable of handling all types of bookings, including gate entry and shows. It will include a payment gateway for full automation and provide analytics for better decision-making.
